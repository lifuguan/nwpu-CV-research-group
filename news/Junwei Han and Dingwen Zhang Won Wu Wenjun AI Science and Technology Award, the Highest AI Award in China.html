High-Altitude UAV Ground Observation Technology

High-altitude unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) are emerging as a new ground observation platform. Due to their significant characteristics such as high flight altitude and wide monitoring range, they effectively compensate for the shortcomings of traditional platforms such as manned aircraft and satellites, and are widely used in various fields.

However, high-altitude UAVs still face critical technical bottlenecks when performing ground observation tasks.

Key Achievements

Professor Junwei Han's team at Northwestern Polytechnical University has overcome multiple key technologies and developed a high-altitude UAV ground observation technology system characterized by "high efficiency, fast detection, and accurate recognition".

The project features:

  • Complex System: High technical difficulty with strong innovation
  • Intellectual Property: 35 authorized national invention patents
  • Academic Publications: Over 30 papers published in important domestic and international journals
  • Technical Breakthrough: Overcame key technical bottlenecks including low efficiency, slow detection, and poor recognition in high-altitude long-endurance UAV ground observation tasks
  • International Level: The overall technology has reached international advanced level
