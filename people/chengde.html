程德 De Cheng

Associate Professor

State Key Laboratory of Integrated Services Networks, Xidian University

中文 Chinese

程德是西安电子科技大学综合业务网理论及关键技术国家重点实验室副教授。在此之前，他于2018年至2021年在华为担任首席工程师。他分别于2017年12月和2011年6月获得西安交通大学博士学位和学士学位。2015年10月至2017年4月期间，在卡内基梅隆大学担任联合培养博士生。他的研究兴趣包括计算机视觉、图像处理和机器学习。他在国际期刊和会议上授权/共同授权了30多篇论文，包括TIP、TNNLS、T-Cybernetics、T-CSVT、CVPR、ICCV、IJCAI等。他的代表性工作之一已被引用超过1200次。

English

De Cheng is an associate professor in the State Key Laboratory of Integrated Services Networks (ISN), Xidian University. Before that, he worked as a Principal Engineer in Huawei from 2018 to 2021. He got his Ph.D. and BSc from Xi'an Jiaotong University at Dec. 2017 and June 2011, respectively. From Oct. 2015 to Apr. 2017, he studied as a joint PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University. His research interests include Computer Vision, Image Processing, and Machine Learning. He has authorized/co-authorized over 30 publications in international journals and conferences, including TIP, TNNLS, T-Cybernetics, T-CSVT, CVPR, ICCV, IJCAI, etc. One of his representative works has been cited over 1,200 times.
